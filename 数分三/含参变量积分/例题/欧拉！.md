# 题目
$$ \int_{0}^{1}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt[n]{1-x^{n}}}(\:n>1\:)$$
# 解答
做变换 $t=x^{n},$ 则
$$ \int_{0}^{1}{\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt[n]{1-x^{n}}}}={\frac{1}{n}}\int_{0}^{1}t^{{\frac{1}{n}}-1}\left(\begin{array}{c}{1-t}\\\end{array}\right)^{-{\frac{1}{n}}}\mathrm{d}t={\frac{1}{n}}\mathrm{B}\Big({\frac{1}{n}},1\:-{\frac{1}{n}}\Big)={\frac{\pi}{n\sin{\frac{\pi}{n}}}}.$$

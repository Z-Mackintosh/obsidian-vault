# 对称区间上的定积分
若函数$f(x)$在对称区间$[-a,a](a>o)$上连续，则
$$  
\int_{-a}^{a} f(x) dx = 0 \quad (\text{当 } f(x) \text{ 为奇函数});  
  \int_{-a}^{a} f(x) dx = 2 \int_{0}^{a} f(x) dx \quad (\text{当 } f(x) \text{ 为偶函数});  \int_{-a}^{a} f(x) dx = \int_{0}^{a} [f(x) + f(-x)] dx.$$  

## 例 5
### 例题
求定积分

$$
\int_{-\frac{\pi}{2}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{\sin^2 x}{1 + e^x} \, dx
$$
### 解
注意到原函数非奇非偶

$$
= \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \left( \frac{\sin^2 x}{1 + e^x} + \frac{\sin^2 (-x)}{1 + e^{-x}} \right) \, dx
$$

$$
= \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \left( \frac{\sin^2 x}{1 + e^x} + \frac{e^x \cdot \sin^2 x}{e^x + 1} \right) \, dx
$$

$$
= \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{\sin^2 x \cdot (e^x + 1)}{e^x + 1} \, dx = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin^2 x \, dx = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{\pi}{2} = \frac{\pi}{4}
$$
##  **华莱士公式**（点火）
  $$\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin^{m} x \cdot \cos^{n} x \, dx = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \cos^{m} x \cdot \sin^{n} x \, dx= \begin{cases} 
   \dfrac{(m-1)!! \cdot (n-1)!!}{(m+n)!!}, & m,n \text{ 不全为偶数} \\[10pt]
   \dfrac{(m-1)!! \cdot (n-1)!!}{(m+n)!!}. \frac{\pi}{2}, & m,n \text{ 全为偶数}
   \end{cases}
   (m, n \in N^{*})$$ 
# 区间再现公式
- 用于变限积分换元问题，方程问题
- $$  
 \int_a^b f(x) dx = \int_a^b f(a+b-x) dx = \frac{1}{2} \int_a^b [f(x) + f(a+b-x)] dx.  
 $$
# 三角函数积分变换公式
- $$  
 \int_{0}^{\pi/2} f(\sin x) dx = \int_{0}^{\pi/2} f(\cos x) dx;  
 $$
 - $$  
\int_{0}^{\pi} f(\sin x) dx = 2 \int_{0}^{\pi/2} f(\sin x) dx, \quad \int_{0}^{\pi} f(\cos x) dx = 2 \int_{0}^{\pi/2} f(\cos x) dx;  
$$
- $$  
 \int_{0}^{2\pi} \sin^n x dx = \int_{0}^{2\pi} \cos^n x dx = \begin{cases} 4 \int_{0}^{\pi/2} \sin^n x dx, & n \text{ 为正偶数} \\ 0, & n \text{ 为正奇数}. \end{cases}  
 $$
 - $$
\int_{0}^{\pi} x f(\sin x) dx = \frac{\pi}{2} \int_{0}^{\pi} f(\sin x) dx
$$
# 周期函数
如果函数 $g(t)$ 是连续的周期函数，周期为 $T$，那么

$$
\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{1}{x} \int_{a}^{x} g(t) dt = \frac{1}{T} \int_{0}^{T} g(t) dt
$$
